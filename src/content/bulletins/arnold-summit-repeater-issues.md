The Arnold Summit repeater is currently not operating consistently. Until further notice, we will be using **simplex check-ins on the hour on channel 20**.

## Road Closures

As of 1:30 PM on February 18, **Hwy 4 is closed** at Avery and by the Rocket station.

- **Many trees and power lines are down** throughout the area
- **Snow plows** are not expected to be clearing side roads for some time

## What You Can Do

- **Monitor channel 20** for simplex check-ins on the hour
- **Stay off the roads** if possible — conditions are hazardous
- **Keep your radios charged** and ready for updates
- **Check back here** for status updates on the repeater and road conditions
